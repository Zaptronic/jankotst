    I design with pixels, code & love.

    I believe the best way to make online products is by working in a small multi-disciplinary team that works in small steps towards measurable goals. I believe by working this way the products created really matter to the user. It's the designer's job to be the central point in the conversation between management, users and development.

    I love simple clear design that stands out and works great.

    I’m much more then just a designer. The services I offer include:

    • Design consultancy
    • Art direction
    • UX design (based on Lean UX)
    • User interface design
    • Web design
    • Icon design
    • Front-end design/ development
